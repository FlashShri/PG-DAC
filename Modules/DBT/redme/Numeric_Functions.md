## Power function
```SQL
mysql> SELECT POW(2, 3) , POW( 3, 3 ) , POW( 2, 0) , POW(2,-2) , POW(2, 0.5) as sqrt ;
+-----------+-------------+------------+-----------+--------------------+
| POW(2, 3) | POW( 3, 3 ) | POW( 2, 0) | POW(2,-2) | sqrt               |
+-----------+-------------+------------+-----------+--------------------+
|         8 |          27 |          1 |      0.25 | 1.4142135623730951 |
+-----------+-------------+------------+-----------+--------------------+
```

## FLOOR() Function
- returs immediately smaller whole no (int)
```SQL
mysql> SELECT Floor(3.56) ,floor(4) , Floor( - 3.56 ) , floor(-1) , floor( - 0.7) ;
+-------------+----------+-----------------+-----------+---------------+
| Floor(3.56) | floor(4) | Floor( - 3.56 ) | floor(-1) | floor( - 0.7) |
+-------------+----------+-----------------+-----------+---------------+
|           3 |        4 |              -4 |        -1 |            -1 |
+-------------+----------+-----------------+-----------+---------------+
```
## Ceiling() function
- returns immediatelty higher whole no
```SQL
mysql> SELECT CEIL( 4.66) , CEIL(4.1) ,CEILING(2), CEILING( -3.4 ) , CEILING( -2.99999) , CEIL( -1)  ;
+-------------+-----------+------------+-----------------+--------------------+-----------+
| CEIL( 4.66) | CEIL(4.1) | CEILING(2) | CEILING( -3.4 ) | CEILING( -2.99999) | CEIL( -1) |
+-------------+-----------+------------+-----------------+--------------------+-----------+
|           5 |         5 |          2 |              -3 |                 -2 |        -1 |
+-------------+-----------+------------+-----------------+--------------------+-----------+
```
## ROUND() function

```SQL
mysql> SELECT Round(2.499)  , Round(2.5) , Round(2.511);
+--------------+------------+--------------+
| Round(2.499) | Round(2.5) | Round(2.511) |
+--------------+------------+--------------+
|            2 |          3 |            3 |
+--------------+------------+--------------+
```
- we can round up to some point also
```SQL
mysql> SELECT Round(2.499 , 2)  , Round(2.5 , 3) , Round(2.511 , 2);
+------------------+----------------+------------------+
| Round(2.499 , 2) | Round(2.5 , 3) | Round(2.511 , 2) |
+------------------+----------------+------------------+
|             2.50 |            2.5 |             2.51 |
+------------------+----------------+------------------+
```

```SQL
mysql> SELECT Round( -1.222) , Round( -1.555)  , Round( 2.34434234213 , 5) ;
+----------------+----------------+---------------------------+
| Round( -1.222) | Round( -1.555) | Round( 2.34434234213 , 5) |
+----------------+----------------+---------------------------+
|             -1 |             -2 |                   2.34434 |
+----------------+----------------+---------------------------+
```
```SQL
mysql> SELECT Round( 4.123456 , 5) ;
-- here rounding upto 5 places
-- 0.12345 but after this 6 is there
-- now num at 5th pos is 5 and 
-- num at 6th pos is 6 
-- 6 > 5 so it will increment the 5th num
+----------------------+
| Round( 4.123456 , 5) |
+----------------------+
|              4.12346 |
+----------------------+

mysql> SELECT Round( 4.654321 , 5) ;
+----------------------+
| Round( 4.654321 , 5) |
+----------------------+
|              4.65432 |
+----------------------+
-- here num at 6th < num at 5th 
-- so no increament
```
```SQL
-- if we take 0 as pos then it will check only first digit after point
-- first digit == 0 --> no change
-- first digit < 5 --> no change
-- first digit >= 5 --> increament by one
mysql> SELECT Round( 4.654321 , 0) , Round( 4.001 , 0 ) , Round( 4.1 , 0) , Round(4.499 , 0) , Round(4.55 , 0) , Round( 4.666);
+----------------------+--------------------+-----------------+------------------+-----------------+---------------+
| Round( 4.654321 , 0) | Round( 4.001 , 0 ) | Round( 4.1 , 0) | Round(4.499 , 0) | Round(4.55 , 0) | Round( 4.666) |
+----------------------+--------------------+-----------------+------------------+-----------------+---------------+
|                    5 |                  4 |               4 |                4 |               5 |             5 |
+----------------------+--------------------+-----------------+------------------+-----------------+---------------+
```
